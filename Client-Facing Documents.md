# How to Use This During Negotiation

When talking to a client:

Step 1
Ask their **goal**

Example:

> Are you looking for visibility growth, a website rebuild, or a platform?

Step 2
Identify **budget range**

Example:

> Most businesses invest between **60k and 120k monthly** for growth.

Step 3
Offer the correct tier.

Never start with the cheapest.

# Important Strategy Tip

Most agencies fail because they **sell services instead of solutions**.

Instead of saying:

> SEO + Website + AI

**Growth Infrastructure**

or

**Digital Growth System**

Clients buy **outcomes**, not tasks.
