# **Productized Solution Pricing Model**.

This is an **internal strategic framework** that helps you structure offers so projects naturally land in the **500k–2M PKR range instead of 100k**.

This is **separate from Pricing Negotiation and Proposal**, but it powers both.

---

# Genuine Optimum

# Productized Solution Pricing Model

*(Internal Strategic Framework)*

---

# 1. The Core Principle

Do **not sell services individually**.

Instead of selling:

* SEO
* Website
* App
* AI

Bundle them into **business solutions**.

Clients pay more when they buy **a result**, not **tasks**.

Example:

❌ Selling
SEO + Website

✔ Selling
**Digital Growth System**

---

# 2. Productized Solution Structure

Each offer should combine **3 layers**.

```
Infrastructure
Growth Engine
Automation
```

---

# 3. Solution Packages

## Package 1 — Digital Foundation

*(Entry System)*

Target Client:

* Small businesses
* startups
* service providers

Includes:

Website Infrastructure

* Business website
* Landing pages
* CMS setup

SEO Foundation

* SEO audit
* On-page optimization
* technical fixes

Analytics Setup

* tracking
* reporting

Investment:

**PKR 120k – 250k**

---

## Package 2 — Growth Engine

*(Most common deal size)*

Target Client:

* growth businesses
* ecommerce brands
* service companies

Includes:

Everything in Digital Foundation +

SEO Growth System

* content strategy
* link building
* local SEO

Conversion Optimization

* landing page improvements
* analytics insights

Automation Tools

* lead capture
* CRM integrations

Investment:

**PKR 350k – 700k**

---

## Package 3 — Digital Infrastructure System

*(High-value system build)*

Target Client:

* scale-up businesses
* funded startups
* established companies

Includes:

Everything in Growth Engine +

Custom Platform Development

* SaaS
* internal tools
* automation dashboards

Advanced Analytics

* business dashboards
* data pipelines

AI Automation

* AI assistants
* workflow automation

Investment:

**PKR 700k – 2M**

---

# 4. Enterprise Systems

Target Client:

* large organizations
* enterprise businesses

Solutions:

Enterprise AI Systems
Enterprise SaaS Platforms
Data Infrastructure
Automation Ecosystems

Investment:

**2M – 10M+ PKR**

---

# 5. How This Increases Deal Size

Example:

### Traditional Agency Sale

Client asks for:

Website

You sell:

Website
Price: **80k**

---

### Productized System Sale

Client asks for:

Website

You respond:

"We recommend a **Digital Foundation System** that includes website infrastructure, SEO setup, and analytics."

Price:

**200k**

---

Different positioning.

---

# 6. Deal Size Ladder

Your offers should naturally guide clients upward.

```
Foundation
120k – 250k

Growth Engine
350k – 700k

Infrastructure System
700k – 2M

Enterprise
2M – 10M+
```

Clients choose **based on scale**, not price.

---

# 7. Sales Conversation Framework

Never start with price.

First ask:

1️⃣ What is the main goal?

* leads
* automation
* platform
* scaling

2️⃣ What systems currently exist?

3️⃣ What is the expected growth?

Then present:

> "Based on your situation, the **Growth Engine System** is the best fit."

Not:

> SEO + Website

---

# 8. Proposal Positioning

In proposals, the sections become:

1. Business Problem
2. Proposed System
3. Implementation Phases
4. Deliverables
5. Investment

Never present **a list of services**.

Always present **a system**.

---

# 9. Internal Offer Rule

Every offer must include at least:

```
1 Infrastructure component
1 Growth component
1 Automation component
```

Example:

Website
SEO
Automation

This instantly pushes deals above **300k**.

---

# 10. Why This Model Works

Businesses buy:

* growth
* systems
* infrastructure

They do **not buy tasks**.

Selling systems creates:

* larger deals
* clearer positioning
* higher perceived expertise

---

# Summary

Pricing negotiation = how you discuss price
Proposal = how you present the offer
Productized solution model = how you **structure the offer itself**

This model turns:

**100k deals → 500k–2M deals**

without changing your services.
